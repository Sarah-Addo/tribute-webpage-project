Tyrion Lannister

    Here is a time line of Tyrion's life.

  • 273 AC - as the third-born child to Lord Tywin and Lady Joanna Lannister. Joanna died giving birth to him, and as a result his father blames him for her death.
  • 286 AC - when he became a man at sixteen, he wanted to take a tour of the nine Free Cities, as his uncles Gerion and Tygett had done. Tywin forbade him to go, and instead, gave him charge of all the cisterns and drains at Casterly Rock.
  • 300 AC - Tyrion visits the nightswatch and befriends Jon Snow and promises to watch over Bran.
  • 301 AC - Tyrion is accused of the assassination attempt on Bran and the murder of Lysa Arryn's husband Jon. He demands a trial by combat and his champion wins him his freedom.
  • 302 AC - Tyrion gets sent to King's Landing by his father to serve as the hand of the king to to reign in his nephew Joffery. He also disobeys his fathers orders and takes the prostitute Shae with him.
  • 303 AC - Tyrion is forced to marry Sansa Stark. After Joffery dies at his wedding Tyrion is accused by Cersei of murdering Joffery. Tyrion flees the Red Keep.
  • 304 AC - Tyrion ends up as Danerys's advisor and helps her plan her attack on Westeros.
